I'm a front-end web developer with a strong focus on web user interfaces that work for everyone and just make sense. I love the unique mixture of creative expression and analytical problem-solving that being a web developer allows me to express. I'm well-versed in front-end technologies including HTML5/CSS, SASS, Javascript, jQuery, Backbone.js, and EmberJS. I'm also a front-end engineering graduate of The Iron Yard Academy.
